Our Project

We purchase carbon credits from renewable energy project developers across South East Asia (Solar, Wind, Hydro). These projects are verified by Certified Bodies (CBs) to meet the highest standards.

15 MW Solar Plant of Westview

The 15 MW Solar Plant of Westview is a solar photovoltaic power project located in the upper state of Westview, NJ. The project has collectively installed and now operates more than 63,000 solar photovoltaic modules with an annual estimated power generation of more than 24,000 megawatt hours.

The Arrakis III Wind Farm Project

The Wind Farm Project is a large-scale wind project in Dune Province and in Atreides Province in Arrakis. The Project has a total installed capacity of 662.5 megawatts.

3 MW Hydro Power Project by Maximoff Power

The Hydro Power Project by Maximoff Power is a 3 megawatt, grid-connected project on the Chthon Stream, which is a tributary of the Earth-616 River.
